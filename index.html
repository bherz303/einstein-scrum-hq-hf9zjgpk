Scrum Scorecard System

A live scoring, tracking, and training system for the 5 CTXH branch Monday boards — turns board hygiene from a gut call into a graded, trend-tracked, coachable standard. Covers the 12-criterion rubric, a manager-facing reference sheet, a live performance dashboard with full scorecard detail per read, and a hands-on training board with automations that mirror the real branch boards. "Done" looks like: every CTXH manager can self-check their board against a public rubric, Brian can grade any branch in minutes with a defensible score, and new managers certify on a mock board before ever touching a live one.
